My history with Computers

Jose Gonzalez 6-23-23

You could say that my history with computers started at a very young age with the Nintendo 64, Although the N64 is an old retro console that does not resemble a typical computer, it carries out functions in a basic manner code in order to produce pixels on a screen that you could play. This sort of technology has always amazed me and through the years I've never lost interest in how computers function. That is why I'm currently pursuing a career in IT as it relates to computers and I hope that in the future I can help others as well as myself understand more about computers and allow people to discover what they can do with them as well as improve their use with them.
